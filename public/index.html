Get to know Thulani



My friends and peers call me “Tools” which is drawn
from my name Thulani. I would like to assume that
they see me as a ‘toolbox’ that contains "Full-Stack" instruments
to develop, design and fix code in a peculiar way.
I am a natural problem solver, from chess and competitive
board games to Rubik’s cubes – this is actually where
my love for code derives from.

I tend to see myself as a versatile Software Developer
since I learn new technologies constantly. I have been exposed
to a wide variety of technologies in my career. I have therefore
refined my strengths throughout my experience. With this said,
I consider myself to be very adaptable, a plug and play kind of person.

Apart from coding, I like to spread laughter, knowledge, and
positive energy. I would warmly welcome the chance to
provide further insight into my knowledge of design, development and
engineering skills as well as technical abilities, personal attributes to
start a track record of success into building revenue generating solutions
compatible across multiple platforms, browsers and devices.
